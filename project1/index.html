At least 24 dead in Los Angeles wildfires

The L.A. fires are most likely the second most destructive in California history according to CNN.

Over 92,000 L.A. County residents have evacuated and 89,000 are sitting under an evacuation warning.

Governor Gavin Newsom stated that these wildfires "could be the worst natural disaster in U.S. history" in terms of costs, scale and scope.

"We're all in this together. We're all better off when we're working together to take care of people and to make sure people are supported," Newsom said.
